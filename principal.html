(Prof.) Mrs. Viji C. MSc (Nursing), BSc (Nursing)

Principal, Brite College of Nursing Bangalore

Principal's Message

Brite College of Nursing sciences have emerged as a premier organization committed towards providing quality education. It has passed many mile stones logged in numerous achievements, created an image for imparting superior education, carved out a niche for itself in Nursing Profession as well as in academic arena.

As an educational institution, our mission is to prepare and develop the right human capital with optimum blend of personal & professional competencies.A commitment of excellence inteaching, research, service and leadership is the essence of our programmes.The educational institutions which have achieved and acclaimed academic excellence cater to the educational needs of thousands of students coming from different parts of India and also abroad.

The college has an excellent physical facilities including labs, well equipped library, classrooms, offices and AV Aids room for various nursing programmes. Our students also have access to the library which has a very vast collection of reference books. Practical experience is provided in multi specialty hospitals like Seventh day Adventist ,Hosmat ,Manipal ,Spandana psychiatric hospital , BOSH hospital, Cloud 9 hospital . For experience in specialized field students are also given exposure to super specialities in renowned hospital like HCG hospital.

Apart from the best teaching faculty, the students are taught by highly qualified nursing professors and doctors of different specialities. Our college also subscribes various foreign as well as Indian journals and books in different specialities for ready reference to the students. Hostel facilities with modern amenities are available to our students. All the facilities for education, extracurricular and sports activities are available to the students. The atmosphere in the college is very congenial for professional growth.

Prof. VIJI C. M.Sc. (Cardiovascular and Thoracic Nursing), B.Sc(Nursing) Principal, BRITE COLLEGE OF NURSING, BANGALORE
